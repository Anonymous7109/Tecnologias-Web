How to Play


This computorized version of the NIM game allows you to play online against other players or offline against the computer.

The game consists on removing tokens and who removes the last token wins!

Rules

  • Each player has its turn to remove the tokens that we want from the same pile.
  • The player need to take at least one token.
  • The game ends when all the piles are empty and the player who last play made that happen is the winner!
